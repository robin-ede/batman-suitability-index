The Problem

Batman needs a new Batcave — but not just any location. We need a site that balances three tactical imperatives:

🎯

Rapid Access to Crime Hotspots

Statistically significant clusters where intervention matters most, identified via Getis-Ord Gi*.

🏙️

High Population Density

Maintaining a strong urban presence within Chicago's most densely populated zones.

🕵️

Strategic Seclusion

Near enough to monitor police infrastructure, yet hidden within industrial corridors for base secrecy.

Methodology & BSI Score

The Batman Suitability Index (BSI) is computed for every H3 hex cell in Chicago by weighting four normalized components:

BSI = 0.35 · Crime Gi* + 0.30 · Pop Density + 0.20 · Police Sweet Spot + 0.15 · Industrial

Getis-Ord Gi* distinguishes true spatial clusters from random noise. Hot spots signal where crime is concentrated beyond chance.

Reproducibility & Ethics

Our entire pipeline is automated. Run the analysis from raw data to final maps with one command:

python run.py

We use publicly available, open-licensed data. Crime information is aggregated and anonymized. Results are intentionally framed to avoid stigmatizing neighborhoods or making unsupported causal claims.
